I'm a self-employed front-end web developer based in Lebanon, working with companies across the globe. I deliver cutting-edge websites for small and medium businesses, as well as work as an outsourcing partner to various web and design agencies.

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences and I use my skills to help others in the web community, where I write about the latest technologies on high-profile websites and on my blog. I've written for A List Apart, the Opera Developers blog, and Codrops, among others.

My work has been recognized by several large organizations, including Apple, Adobe and Wikipedia.

My website has also been awarded HTML5 Site Of The Month in the September 2013 issue of NET magazine.

I'm a Muslim, an optimist, and an occasional artist. Hobbies include drawing, water color painting, photography, and reading.
